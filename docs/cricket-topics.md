# Cricket Editorial Topics & Taxonomic Mapping

A structured classification of editorial topics is essential for organizing coverage, managing databases, and defining content feeds. This taxonomy maps the primary content categories deployed across our cricket network.

---

## 1. Cricket News
*   **Focus**: Real-time events, match results, injury announcements, board decisions, and administrative changes.
*   **Editorial Requirement**: High immediacy, strict objective reporting, and clear attribution of sources (e.g., ICC, national cricket boards).

## 2. Match Prediction & Forecasting
*   **Focus**: Statistical and environmental analysis prior to matches. Includes analysis of playing XIs, pitch reports, weather, and historical head-to-head records.
*   **Editorial Requirement**: Purely analytical and mathematical; strictly excludes any betting advice or commercial promotion of gambling.

## 3. Team Analysis & Tactical Breakdowns
*   **Focus**: In-depth reviews of team strategies, squad selection depth, batting/bowling combinations, and historic performance trajectories.
*   **Editorial Requirement**: Long-form, expert-driven commentary supported by historical match statistics.

## 4. Player Profiles & Biographies
*   **Focus**: Career histories, statistical milestones, playing styles (e.g., right-arm leg-spin, left-handed batsman), and chronological career progression.
*   **Editorial Requirement**: Biographies must remain objective, well-researched, and focus strictly on professional sports achievements.

## 5. Rankings & Standings
*   **Focus**: Regular updates on ICC team and player rankings across formats (Test, ODI, T20I), tournament points tables, and series standings.
*   **Editorial Requirement**: Accurate reporting of mathematical points and updates immediately following tournament phase completions.

## 6. Match Highlights & Key Moments
*   **Focus**: Summary notes of major match highlights, turning points, crucial overs, and game-changing wickets or partnerships.
*   **Editorial Requirement**: Descriptive, visually structured timelines detailing how a match shifted at critical junctions.

## 7. Records & Milestones
*   **Focus**: Historic feats, such as most career wickets, highest individual batting scores, fastest centuries, and historic team winning streaks.
*   **Editorial Requirement**: Verification against official databases (e.g., ESPNcricinfo, ICC records) before publication.

## 8. Auction & Trading Window
*   **Focus**: Franchise league acquisitions, draft selections, transfer fees, player retentions, and auction budgets (e.g., IPL, PSL, BBL, CPL).
*   **Editorial Requirement**: Clear differentiation between officially confirmed transfers and speculative trade rumors.
